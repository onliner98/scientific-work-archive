5. QA/Post-editing of the translated text  
6. Merging/recreation of the original document in the target language

Level 1 -  Complete conformance: Requires complete implementation of all of the standards and processing models that MUST be implemented exactly as defined
in the OAXAL reference model.  
Level 2 -  Standard conformance: Requires implementation of all standards relevant to a particular process and allows for the omission of any standards
not relevant to that process.  
Level 3 -  Basic conformance: Requires implementation of the standards and processing models that MUST implement a minimum of W3C ITS and XLIFF 
and least one of the following: xml:tm, GMX/V, TMX, SRX, Unicode TR29.
