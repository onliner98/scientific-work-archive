Untersucht Tiefe und Breite von CNNs und Tiefe und Breite von FullyConnectedLayers on Top of CNN
Results:
- Deeper CNNs perform better than shallow models overdeeper datasets and vice versa
- Shallow CNNs need wider/more FC Layers, while deeper CNNs need smaller/less FC layers
- more FC Layers for deeper Datasets(finegrained classes) wider FC Layers for wider Datasets(man broad classes)

Used Architecture: CNN
